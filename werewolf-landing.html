Welcome to Werewolf

A social deduction game where players use observation and strategy to identify the werewolves among them.

Game Overview

Werewolf is a simplified digital adaptation of the classic social deduction game. Players are assigned secret roles as either Villagers or Werewolves. The objective is to identify and eliminate players through democratic voting. Victory comes through strategy, careful observation, and persuasive discussion!

Roles

Werewolf

You secretly work against the village. Your goal is to remain undetected while helping eliminate villagers through the voting process. Use deception and misdirection to avoid being caught!

Villager

Your goal is to identify and eliminate the werewolves through voting. Pay close attention to discussions, behavior patterns, and voting history to determine who might be hiding their true nature.

Game Phases

Night Phase

  • All players close their eyes
  • Werewolves and Villagers sleep through the night (no night elimination)

Day Phase

  • All players open their eyes
  • Players discuss who they suspect might be a werewolf
  • Everyone votes to eliminate one player

How to Win

  • Villagers win by eliminating all werewolves through voting
  • Werewolves win by avoiding detection until they equal or outnumber the villagers
